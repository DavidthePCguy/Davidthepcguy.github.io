Our Purpose

Creative Bakery Solutions, LLC. is a national provider of bakery mix products. CBS supplies wholesale bakery companies with a variety of dry mixes, bases, and concentrates. CBS will also custom blend your formula or ours under your label. We will also formulate products to your specifications.

Our mix is specially formulated to yield high quality, high performance cakes, muffins, brownies, and breads that are rich, moist, and flavorful.

Finest ingredients

Our robust, high performance formulas use only the highest quality ingredients making our products the best in class while offering certain unique bowl cost advantages.

All mixes are packed in 50 lb bags or 2,000 lb totes.
